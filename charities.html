Here at Outer Space, we believe art can be a force for positive change in this world, both individually and collectively.

With this belief, Outer Space donates 5% of our profits generated from each sold artwork to either a local charity in Concord NH or to a larger (inter)national organization of the artists' choosing.

Below are various charities that we have donated proceeds to on behalf of our artists. Thank you for supporting Outer Space, the artists who exhibit here, and these fantastic organizations.

Kimball Jenkins' Mission is to Cultivate Creativity, Make Arts Education Accessible, and Honor Historic Preservation
